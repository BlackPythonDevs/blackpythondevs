# Feb 2026 Leadership Meeting Overview

Meeting Feb 2026

The meeting was led by Jay Miller and covered regional updates, sponsorship activities, corporate partnerships, and community recognition. The council reviewed conference sponsorships across multiple regions, approved the first Distinguished Service Award, and discussed the PSF Community Partner Program. The meeting also addressed student ambassador programs and upcoming technical meetups in Africa.

The meeting opened with discussion of upcoming travel plans. Jay mentioned he would be traveling to Berlin, California, and the Philippines in the coming weeks. Jay discussed ongoing support for Python user groups, including PyTexas and PyToronto, and outlined plans for PyTexas sponsorship, which will allocate funds to grants and user groups. ## Conference Grant Approval Process

The team discussed the process for approving and managing conference grants, including the workflow for reviewing and approving requests and the use of templates for communication with grantees. Jay explained that two people, typically Jay and the regional person, need to approve requests. Kudzayi raised concerns about delays in payments, particularly for PyCon Namibia, and Jay acknowledged that GNOME can be difficult to reach during certain events (Guadec, FOSDEM, and leadership changeover). The team emphasized the importance of addressing diversity in the Python community and considering the accessibility of events for underrepresented groups.
