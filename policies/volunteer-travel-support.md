## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines travel support available to Black Python Devs volunteers attending conferences on behalf of the organization.

## 2. Eligibility

To be eligible for volunteer travel support, you must:

- Be an approved volunteer for a Black Python Devs event or a conference where Black Python Devs has an official presence.
- Have written approval from the Regional Executor responsible for the event's location prior to incurring any travel expenses.

## 3. Travel support cap

- The maximum travel support amount per volunteer per event is **$500 USD**.
- This cap covers all travel-related expenses, including transportation, accommodation, and other approved costs.

## 4. Approval process

All volunteer travel support requests must be approved in advance by the Regional Executor for the event's location. Approval must be obtained in writing before any expenses are incurred.

## 5. How to get reimbursed

Once your travel support has been approved, follow the [Paying for things and seeking reimbursement](/policies/paying-for-things-and-getting-reimbursed.md) policy to submit your expenses and receive reimbursement.
