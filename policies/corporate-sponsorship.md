This policy outlines sponsorship tiers and benefits for corporate sponsors of Black Python Devs.

## Sponsorship tiers

### Full Sponsorship — $10,000 USD or more

Corporate sponsors contributing $10,000 USD or more annually receive the full benefits package outlined below.

### Partial Sponsorship — below $10,000 USD

Sponsors contributing less than $10,000 USD may receive a subset of the benefits listed below, determined at the discretion of the BPD Leadership Team based on the sponsorship amount.

## Sponsor benefits

Full sponsors receive the following benefits:

- **Community channel access**: Sponsors are granted access to BPD community channels to engage directly with members.
- **Announcements**: Sponsors are able to publish announcements for events and campaigns in Authorized channels.
- **Job listings**: Sponsors may post job listings within the BPD community.
- **Logo placement at BPD events**: Sponsor logos will be displayed on sponsorship banners at BPD-organized and BPD-sponsored events.
- **Logo placement at private community events**: Sponsor logos will be displayed at private events within the BPD community.

## Approval process

All corporate sponsorship agreements must be reviewed and approved by the BPD Leadership Team. Sponsorship agreements are managed in coordination with the GNOME Foundation.
