# Program outline template

Since Black Python Devs is a fiscally hosted organization under the GNOME Foundation, as stated in [Article I, Subsection 2 of the Black Python Devs Bylaws](/byelaws/article_i.md#section-2-fiscal-hosting-and-organizational-structure), all new programs require approval from the GNOME Board.

Fill out and submit this template to the GNOME Foundation when notifying them of a new BPD program:

```md
**GNOME Foundation**
# Fiscally Sponsored Project Program Outline

1. Fiscally sponsored project
   [Name of the program]

2. Purpose of program.
   Describe the goals and intended impact of the program.

3. Who is served by the program?
   Who are the target groups who will be assisted by the program, and who will be eligible to apply?

4. Program budget
   What is the total amount of funding that will be made available for the program? Will the budget be limited to a particular time period?

5. Program payments
   How many payments and what amounts?

6. How will the FSP make decisions?
   How will the fiscally sponsored project make decisions about who receives support from the program?

7. How will the FSP manage conflict of interest?
   If a funding recipient has a potential conflict of interest, how will the conflict be handled?

8. Who is not eligible?
   Is there anyone who will not be able to apply for the program?

9. Who manages the program for the FSP?
   Which individuals and groups will be responsible for operating the program?
```
